Step 1: 
First we start with removing the performance-tweaks and gamemode packages from your install. 
pacman -R performance-tweaks gamemode

Step 2: Now that Garuda's tweaks have been removed we will need to install a few packages to manage the CPU and keep it from overheating and crashing the system. 
sudo pacman -S auto-cpufreq thermald
