Achim
In reply to this message
If the Max power output is higher, not much bad can happen, your device will draw as much power as it needs. Efficiency probably is not optimal. Maybe the current is not very stable (which could have some effect on the device, usually touch stuff is affected).

If the charger has to few power (ampere) to support the device, it can overheat and break (and in that process might kill a lot of other things, too, even burn down your house).

It might be that the device knows what the charger can supply and acts accordingly (like with usb chargers), but that is not necessarily the case.

So in any case, check the temperature of the charger from time to time, if you use something new / not officially supported.
