As a rule of thumb all files in your home (i.e. /home/USERNAME/ ) should be yours, i.e. owned by you and you should have full access.

Second rule of thumb: All files *not* in your home should *not* be yours and you should only have read rights (there are some exceptions like /tmp and thumb drives mounted in /media or so).
