Sleep and hibernation are two different things.

Sleep is quite common to not work (usually a black screen after wake up or similar). You might want to try some different kernels or have a look for example in the arch wiki if some know issues are present and fixed/work around for your hardware.

Hibernation should work fine. It does take much more time, though. Before going into hibernation contents of the RAM is stored on disk, then a normal shutdown happens. On "wake up" a normal boot happens until at some point the contents of the RAM are restored from disk.
If your system boots up without issues, then hibernation should be working fine. (but be patient, with some gigs the restore takes some time)
